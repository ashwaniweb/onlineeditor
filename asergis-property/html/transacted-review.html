Description

Our cool and comfortable 2 bedroom apartment with exposed brick has a true village feeling! It comfortably fits four guests and is centrally located on a quiet street in Marascala and is just few meters away from Beach and Centre!

This 4th Floor 2 bedroom Modern Apartment is served with Lift, Fully and highly furnished with new and modern furniture. It comprises 2 Bedrooms 1 with double bed and AC, and the other bedroom with 2 single beds, Kitchen/Living, Bathroom and En-suite with shower, 42 inch Led TV etc.
